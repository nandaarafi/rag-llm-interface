## Key Design Principles

### 1. Visual Hierarchy
- Large, clear headlines
- Important buttons prominently displayed
- Progress indicators throughout flow
- Consistent spacing and typography

### 2. Progress Indication
- Step indicators (1/15, 2/15, etc.)
- Progress bars during processing
- Clear "next steps" guidance

### 3. Psychological Triggers
- Social proof throughout
- Immediate value delivery
- Personal investment (recording)
- Fear of missing out (limited offers)

### 4. Mobile-First Design
- Thumb-friendly button sizes
- Single-column layouts
- Easy text input
- Large recording interface

### 5. Trust Building
- Privacy assurances
- Professional design
- Clear value propositions
- Money-back guarantees

## Conversion Optimization Notes

### A/B Testing Elements
- Free trial length: 3 vs 7 vs 14 days
- Price points: $9.99 vs $14.99 vs $19.99
- Recording topics: intro vs work presentation
- Confidence score ranges: make most people score 6-7/10
- Social proof placement and content

### Success Metrics
- **Completion rate:** 60%+ through confidence test
- **Trial conversion:** 15%+ to free trial
- **Paid conversion:** 25%+ trial to paid subscription
- **Overall conversion:** 2-3% visitor to paid customer

### Drop-off Analysis Points
- Screen 2: Pain point quiz (expect 20% drop)
- Screen 7: Recording request (expect 30% drop)
- Screen 13: First paywall (expect 40% drop)
- Screen 14: Pricing (final conversion point)
