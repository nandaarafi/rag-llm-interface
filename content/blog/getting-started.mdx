# Introduction

Welcome to our AI-powered chatbot platform! This guide will help you get started and make the most of our features.

## What You Can Do

Our chatbot offers a wide range of capabilities:

- **Natural Conversations**: Chat naturally with our AI assistant
- **Code Generation**: Get help with programming and development
- **Document Analysis**: Upload and analyze documents
- **Creative Writing**: Generate creative content and stories

## Key Features

### 1. Smart Conversations

Our AI understands context and maintains conversation history, making interactions feel natural and productive.

### 2. File Uploads

You can upload various file types including:
- Documents (PDF, DOC, TXT)
- Images (JPG, PNG, GIF)
- Code files (JS, PY, HTML, etc.)

### 3. Artifact Generation

Generate and edit various types of content:
- Code snippets
- Text documents
- Presentations
- Spreadsheets

## Getting Started

1. **Sign Up**: Create your account using Google OAuth or email
2. **Start Chatting**: Begin a conversation with our AI assistant
3. **Upload Files**: Drag and drop files to analyze or discuss
4. **Generate Content**: Use our artifact system to create structured content

## Tips for Best Results

- Be specific in your requests
- Provide context when asking questions
- Use the file upload feature for document analysis
- Experiment with different conversation styles

## Need Help?

If you encounter any issues or have questions, don't hesitate to:
- Check our documentation
- Contact our support team
- Join our community forums
